SHOEI GT-Air II

The Shoei GT-Air 2 is a premium full-face motorcycle helmet that offers a combination of style, comfort, and advanced technology. Designed as an all-rounder helmet, it caters to the needs of riders seeking both functionality and aesthetics. One of the standout features of the GT-Air 2 is its aerodynamic design, which has been refined through wind tunnel testing and feedback from professional riders. This design helps to minimize drag, lift, and pressure on the rider's neck, resulting in a stable and comfortable riding experience. The helmet incorporates Shoei's Multi-Ply Matrix AIM shell, which combines fiberglass material with composite fibers and resins to enhance impact resistance. The Multi-Piece/Multi-Density EPS liner further enhances impact absorption and ventilation, providing an additional layer of protection. The GT-Air 2 also features an internal sun shield, allowing riders to quickly adjust to changing light conditions without the need for separate sunglasses. The forehead area of the outer shell is raised slightly to accommodate the internal sun shield while maintaining the integrity of the helmet's inner EPS liner. Safety is a top priority for Shoei, and the GT-Air 2 meets DOT standards. It also exceeds the ANSI Z80.3-2010 standard for non-prescription eyewear, ensuring optimal protection for your eyes. Additionally, the GT-Air 2 incorporates the Emergency Quick Release System (E.Q.R.S.), which allows for easy removal of the helmet in case of an emergency. This system features a special ribbon on the cheek pad that minimizes weight on the rider's neck, facilitating quick and safe helmet removal. The GT-Air 2 comes in various shell sizes to ensure a proper fit for different head sizes. It offers compatibility with the GT-Air II specific SRL 2 Bluetooth Communication System by Sena, allowing for seamless integration of communication technology. Overall, the Shoei GT-Air 2 is a well-crafted helmet that combines safety, comfort, and style. Its advanced features and attention to detail make it a popular choice among riders looking for a high-quality helmet for their motorcycle adventures.
