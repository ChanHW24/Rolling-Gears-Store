SHOEI GT-Air II

Product Details:

The Shoei GT-Air 2 is a premium full-face motorcycle helmet that offers style, comfort, and advanced technology. It has an aerodynamic design that minimizes drag and pressure on the rider's neck. The helmet is made with a Multi-Ply Matrix AIM shell, which enhances impact resistance. It also features a Multi-Piece/Multi-Density EPS liner for added impact absorption and ventilation. The GT-Air 2 has an internal sun shield and meets safety standards, including DOT and ANSI Z80.3-2010 for eye protection. It has an Emergency Quick Release System for easy helmet removal in emergencies. The helmet comes in various shell sizes and is compatible with a Bluetooth communication system. Overall, the Shoei GT-Air 2 is a popular choice for riders seeking a high-quality helmet that combines safety, comfort, and style. For more detailed information, it is recommended to refer to the official Shoei website or the product manual.
